# Handvantage: Website Optimization Guide
**Goal:** Transform `pages.handvantage.com` from a "Vendor" page to a "Pilot" Command Center.

---

## 1. The Hero Section (Above the Fold)
**Current:** "We don't just watch the radar. We fly the plane 24/7." (Good copy, weak visual).
**The Upgrade:**
*   **Visual:** Use **`Handvantage_Ad_Brand_Pilot_Square.png`** (The Cockpit View) as the Hero Background or Split-Screen Image.
*   **Headline:** "The Pilot, Not The Plane."
*   **Sub-Headline:** "Most MSPs sell you tools. We provide the elite pilots to fly them. 24/7 Autonomous SOC for the price of an intern."
*   **Primary CTA:** **[DEPLOY THE FLEET]** (Solid Button, Electric Blue).
*   **Secondary CTA:** **[CALCULATE BUSINESS DRAG]** (Outline Button).

## 2. The "Dual Squeeze" Section (The Problem)

**The Upgrade:**

    *   **Caption:** "THE SILENCE: The risk you can't see."

---

## 3. The "Fleet" Section (The Solution)
**Current:** Agent Cards (Hunter, Sentry, Concierge).
**The Upgrade:** Keep the cards, but sharpen the copy.
*   **The Hunter:** Change "I book meetings" to **"I hunt threats and opportunities 24/7."**
*   **The Sentry:** Change "I watch logs" to **"I filter the noise. You only see the signal."**
*   **The Concierge:** Change "I solve tickets" to **"I resolve friction in milliseconds."**

---

## 4. The "Math" Section (The Closer)
**Current:** Comparison Table ($180k vs. Fraction).
**The Upgrade:** Make it a **Calculator**, not just a table.
*   **Headline:** "The $180k Problem."
*   **Visual:** A simple bar chart comparison.
    *   **Bar 1 (Red):** Internal Hire ($180k + Benefits + Burnout).
    *   **Bar 2 (Green):** Handvantage Fleet (Fraction of Cost + 24/7).
*   **CTA:** **[SEE YOUR SAVINGS]** (Links to ROI Calculator).
