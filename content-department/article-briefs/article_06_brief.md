> ## 3. Key Insights from Research
> 
> - **The Relationship:** An Identity Fabric is the architectural foundation that makes a Zero Trust strategy possible. It integrates and orchestrates multiple IAM systems to act as a single, unified system.
> - **The Problem it Solves:** It addresses the issue of fragmented identity silos, which 70% of organizations say is a root cause of cybersecurity risk. It provides the consistent identity data needed for the Policy Decision Point (PDP) in a Zero Trust architecture.
> - **Key Capabilities:** An Identity Fabric provides capabilities like directory services, adaptive access, decentralized identity management, and entitlement management.
> - **The Human and Machine Problem:** A modern Identity Fabric must manage both human and machine identities, which are expected to double in 2025.
