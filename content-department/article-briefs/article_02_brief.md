> ## 3. Key Insights from Research
> 
> - **SAIL (Security Analytics & Intelligence Layer):** This is the analytic brain that extends traditional SIEM/XDR. It aggregates telemetry, uses machine learning for dynamic risk scoring, and enables predictive defense. Organizations with extensive security AI/automation in their analytics layer identify and contain breaches 80 days faster.
> - **PPPM (Policy, Posture, and Playbook Management):** This is the governance hub that acts as the authoritative control plane. It translates high-level policies (e.g., NIST, ZTA) into configurations for vendor tools and provides automation guardrails to prevent business disruption.
> - **IML (Infrastructure Management Layer):** This layer provides the asset context, including device health and configuration state, which feeds into the SAIL for richer analysis.
> - **Integration Standards:** Emphasize the importance of open standards like OCSF (Open Cybersecurity Schema Framework) for data normalization, which is critical for SAIL to function effectively.
