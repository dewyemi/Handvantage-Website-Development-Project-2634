> ## 3. Key Insights from Research
> 
> - **The Need for a Lifecycle Approach:** Machine identities (API keys, certificates, tokens) are ephemeral and require a full lifecycle management approach, not just a one-time setup.
> - **Create:** Securely provision new machine identities with defined ownership and purpose.
> - **Onboard:** Register the identity in a central inventory or vault.
> - **Lock down:** Apply the principle of least privilege, granting only the necessary permissions for the specific task.
> - **Observe:** Continuously monitor the identity for anomalous behavior (e.g., usage outside of expected times or locations).
> - **Rotate:** Automatically rotate credentials at regular intervals or after a potential compromise.
