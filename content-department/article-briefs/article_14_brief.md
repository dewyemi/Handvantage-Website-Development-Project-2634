> ## 3. Key Insights from Research
> 
> - **The New Kill Chain:** The modern ransomware kill chain includes a critical new step: **Backup Discovery and Destruction**. Attackers know that if they can eliminate the backups, they can force a ransom payment.
> - **The 57% Data Loss Problem:** 57% of organizations that suffer a ransomware attack recover less than 50% of their data. This is a direct result of attackers successfully destroying backups.
> - **How They Do It:** Attackers use legitimate administrative tools (Living Off the Land techniques) to find and delete backup files, reconfigure backup jobs, and disable backup software.
> - **The Time Factor:** The time between initial compromise and the attack on backups can be just hours. This requires an automated and proactive defense.
