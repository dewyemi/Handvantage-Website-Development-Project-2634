## 3. Key Insights from Research

- **The Problem:** CSMA addresses the "castles of disconnected controls" problem, where siloed security tools lead to integration fatigue and alert overload. The average SOC faces 960 alerts per day, with 40% going uninvestigated.
- **The Cost of Inaction:** Organizations take an average of 241 days to identify and contain a breach. Faster responders (under 200 days) save over $1.14 million per breach.
- **The 4 Layers:** Introduce the four foundational layers of CSMA as defined by Gartner: Security Analytics and Intelligence (SAIL), Distributed Identity Fabric, Consolidated Policy and Posture Management (PPPM), and Consolidated Dashboards.
- **Business Enablement:** Frame CSMA not just as a security strategy, but as a business enabler that supports digital acceleration, hybrid work, and the use of third-party services.
