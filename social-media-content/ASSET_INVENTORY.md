## Usage Workflow

### For Daily Posting:
1. Consult the content calendar to identify the post for the day
2. Open the corresponding pillar content file
3. Copy the post content
4. Download the corresponding graphic from the social-media-content directory
5. Post to LinkedIn or X with the graphic attached
6. Monitor engagement and respond to comments

### For Video Content:
1. Open `heygen_video_scripts.md`
2. Select the appropriate script for the pillar
3. Use HeyGen to generate the AI avatar video with the script
4. Download the video
5. Post to LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, or other platforms
6. Pair with the corresponding graphic and text content

### For Customization:
1. All Canva designs are editable using the Canva Design IDs
2. Log into Canva account
3. Search for the design by ID or name
4. Make necessary edits
5. Export as PNG for social media use

## Brand Consistency Guidelines

All assets follow the OLAYEMIS brand identity:

**Voice:** Authoritative yet approachable, pragmatic, vendor-neutral
**Tone:** Concise, insightful, generous with knowledge
**Visual Style:** Modern, professional, cybersecurity-themed
**Color Palette:** Blues, purples, greens, oranges (depending on pillar)
**Typography:** Clean, professional fonts
**Imagery:** Abstract tech graphics, no stock photos of people

## Success Metrics to Track

For each post, track:
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers
- **Click-Through Rate:** Clicks on links / Total impressions
- **Follower Growth:** New followers per week
- **Community Mentions:** Times Olayemi is tagged or mentioned
- **Video Views:** For video content
- **Comments Quality:** Depth and relevance of discussions
